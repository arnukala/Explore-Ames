FAQ

Questions & Answers

Find helpful answers about Explore Ames, dining, and local experiences.

What is Explore Ames?
Explore Ames is a student-created guide that highlights local diners, shops, and attractions around Ames, Iowa.

Who manages the website?
It’s built and maintained by the COM S 3190 Web Development student team at Iowa State University.

Can I place orders online?
Currently, Explore Ames is an informational website and does not process payments or orders directly.

Will future updates include e-commerce?
Yes, future versions may integrate secure checkout options for local products and souvenirs.

How does pickup work?
For future shop items, users will be able to select pickup points at listed Ames locations.

Do you offer shipping?
Shipping options are not yet available but are being planned for upcoming releases.

Can I return a product?
Once the shop feature launches, products can be returned within 7 days of pickup.

Are there any conditions?
Items must be unused and in original packaging. Refunds will be processed through the original payment method.

What if I need urgent help in Ames?
For emergencies, call 911. For campus-related help, contact ISU Police at (515) 294-4428.

Can I reach Explore Ames team?
Yes, you can reach the student team via email at exploreames@iastate.edu.
